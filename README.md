# 📖 About the Prototype

**PartnerIQ** is an **AI-powered employee learning platform prototype** designed to reimagine how café employees learn, practice, and build confidence during onboarding and daily operations.

This project explores how Artificial Intelligence can centralize operational knowledge into a single platform that provides instant guidance, personalized learning, adaptive assessments, and continuous skill development.

> **Note:** This is an independent portfolio prototype created to demonstrate product thinking, UI/UX design, and frontend development skills. It is **not an official Starbucks application**.

---

# 💡 Inspiration

The idea behind PartnerIQ originated from my experience working as a **Barista at Starbucks**.

While working on the floor, I observed that new partners often relied on experienced team members to answer repetitive operational questions.

Some of the most common questions included:

- How many syrup pumps go into each beverage?
- What is the shelf life of a product?
- Which coffee roast has which flavor profile?
- How should a beverage be prepared correctly?
- What topics should I revise before my next shift?

Although training resources were available, information was scattered across recipe cards, manuals, and verbal guidance from experienced partners.

This often resulted in:

- Slower onboarding
- Repeated interruptions during busy shifts
- Inconsistent learning experiences
- Reduced confidence among new employees

These real workplace observations inspired me to imagine a centralized AI-powered learning platform that could provide instant operational guidance while helping employees continuously improve their knowledge.

That idea eventually evolved into **PartnerIQ**.

---

# 🎯 Problem Statement

Employee onboarding and operational training often face several challenges:

- Learning resources are scattered across multiple materials.
- New employees frequently depend on senior team members.
- Training quality varies from store to store.
- There is no personalized learning experience.
- Employees have limited visibility into their progress.

---

# 🚀 Proposed Solution

PartnerIQ brings learning, operational guidance, and AI assistance together into one centralized platform.

Employees can:

- Learn beverage recipes
- Access shelf-life information
- Explore coffee origins and roast profiles
- Practice coffee tasting
- Take adaptive knowledge assessments
- Receive personalized AI recommendations
- Track their learning progress
- Prepare for upcoming shifts
